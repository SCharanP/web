Addendum

The Lights Out game was originally created by Tiger Electronics in 1995. It is a puzzle game where the goal is to switch off all the lights on a board of lights. Each light has two states: on and off. Clicking on a light toggles its state and the state of its adjacent lights (up, down, left, right). The game is known for its challenging yet addictive gameplay.

Key game development steps:

  • Game Mechanics: Each click toggles the clicked light and its immediate neighbors, which requires strategy and planning to solve the puzzle.
  • Solvable Setup: Randomly toggling lights on the board ensures a solvable starting point for players.
  • User Experience: The game design emphasizes clarity and simplicity, allowing players to focus on the puzzle.
  • Feedback and Sound: Visual and auditory feedback provide satisfying interactions for players.

Enjoy playing the Lights Out game! Practice solving puzzles and improve your strategy to tackle increasingly difficult boards.
